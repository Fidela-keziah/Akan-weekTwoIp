AKAN Naming, an interesting tradition in GHANA!

The Akan people of Ghana frequently name their children after the day of the week they were born and the order in which they were born. These "day names" have further meanings concerning the soul and character of the person .children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born.
